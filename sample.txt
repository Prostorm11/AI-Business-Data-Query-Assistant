Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI includes areas such as machine learning, natural language processing, robotics, and computer vision. RAG systems are AI pipelines designed to retrieve relevant information from a large knowledge base and generate answers or summaries based on context. By combining retrieval and generative capabilities, these systems can answer questions accurately, provide explanations, and assist with decision-making in various industries.
